Our Services

At John's Phone and Accessories, we offer a range of services to enhance your experience with our products:

Product Consultation

Our knowledgeable staff are here to assist you in finding the
perfect phone, laptop, or accessory to meet your needs. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a first-time buyer,
we'll provide personalized recommendations and expert advice.

Device Repairs

Accidents happen, but we're here to help. Our skilled technicians can diagnose and repair a wide range of issues,
from cracked screens to malfunctioning hardware. With fast turnaround times and competitive prices,
you can trust us to get your device back up and running in no time.

Trade-In Program

Ready to upgrade your device? Take advantage of our trade-in program and receive credit towards your new purchase.
Simply bring in your old phone or laptop, and we'll provide a fair appraisal and apply the
value towards your new device.

Accessories Installation

Enhance your device with our range of accessories, from protective cases to high-quality headphones.
Our experts can assist with installation and setup to ensure that your accessories are working seamlessly with your device.
